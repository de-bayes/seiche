How the published forecasts actually did.

Every forecast we publish is logged the moment it ships and scored against what the lake actually did once each hour resolved. Nothing here is cherry-picked, and the numbers move with the weather: the log records the real published quantiles, so it captures weather-forecast error too, not just the model under perfect weather.
